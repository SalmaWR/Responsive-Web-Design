Malcolm X Timeline

  • May 19, 1925: Malcolm Little (later known as Malcolm X) is born in Omaha, Nebraska.
  • 1931: Malcolm's father, Earl Little, dies, possibly murdered by white supremacists.
  • 1946: Malcolm is sentenced to prison for burglary. While in prison, he converts to the Nation of Islam.
  • 1952: Malcolm is released from prison and becomes a minister and national spokesperson for the Nation of Islam.
  • 1958: Malcolm marries Betty Sanders (later Betty Shabazz).
  • 1964: Malcolm X leaves the Nation of Islam and goes on a pilgrimage to Mecca, where he converts to Sunni Islam and changes his name to El-Hajj Malik El-Shabazz.
  • February 21, 1965: Malcolm X is assassinated while giving a speech at the Audubon Ballroom in New York City.
